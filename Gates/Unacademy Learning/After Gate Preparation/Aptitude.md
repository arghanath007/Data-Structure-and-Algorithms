* Odd - Even -> 2 - 2 -> 0
* 121 is divisible by **11**.
* 11 ^ 3 -> 1331
* Odd -> 1 + 3 -> 4
* Even -> 3 + 1 -> 4
* Odd - Even -> 4 - 4 -> 0
* 1331 is divisible by **11**.
* Number -> 517
* Odd -> 7 + 5 -> 12
* Even -> 1
* Odd - Even -> 12 - 1 -> 11
* 517 is divisible by **11**.
